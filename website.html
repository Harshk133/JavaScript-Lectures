Forts of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj

Raigad

Raigad Fort is a hill fort in Maharashtra, India, that was the capital of the Maratha Empire. It's a classic example of Maratha architecture and is known as the "Gibraltar of the east".

Naldurg

Naldurg Fort is a historic fort in the Osmanabad district of Maharashtra, India. It was built in the medieval period by Raja Nalraja. The fort is known for its star-shaped architecture and bastions.

Torna

Torna Fort, also known as Prachandagad, is a large fort in the Pune district of Maharashtra, India. It's a popular trekking destination and one of the highest hill forts in the state.

Pratapgad

Pratapgad Fort is a mountain fort in the Satara district of Maharashtra, India. It was built by Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj in 1656. The fort is a popular tourist destination.

Harihar

Harihar Fort is a historic fort in the Nashik district of Maharashtra, India. It's known for its rock-cut steps and is a popular destination for hikers.

Lohagad

Lohagad Fort, also known as the Iron Fort, is a stone fort in Maharashtra, India. It's a popular trekking destination and a significant historical site.

Sindhudurg

Sindhudurg Fort is a historic sea fort in the Arabian Sea, built by Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj. It's located in the Sindhudurg district of Maharashtra, India.

Vijaydurg

Vijaydurg Fort is a historic fort in the Sindhudurg district of Maharashtra, India. It was built during the Shilahar dynasty and was captured by Shivaji Maharaj in 1653.

Shivneri

Shivneri Fort is a 17th-century military fort in the Pune district of Maharashtra, India. It's the birthplace of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj, the founder of the Maratha Empire.
